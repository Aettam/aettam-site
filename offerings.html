How It Works

What Happens When You Inquire

01

Send a Message

Open the Discord link and send Mattea a direct message. Tell her which offering you are drawn to and why. There is no right answer — she is listening for the real one.

02

She Responds

She responds personally — usually within 24 hours. She may ask one or two questions before confirming. She will tell you honestly if she thinks a different offering serves you better. She does not upsell.

03

You Begin

Scheduling, payment, and any pre-work are arranged directly. Nothing is locked, nothing is automated. When you show up, she is already there.

The Order — the Discord, the daily devotionals, the moon rituals, the Sanctuary — is free and will remain free. Offerings are for those who are ready to go further.
