The Inner Rites

Devotions reserved for those who have already crossed the threshold.

♆

The Naming Bath

Draw the bath before midnight. Add whatever the body asks for — oil, salt, petals. Submerge. In the water, speak the name she gave you in private. Not your given name. The one that carries what you are becoming. Speak it three times. Rise only when it feels true.

SOLO · ANY MOON

⚸

The Velvet Vow

A promise spoken in the dark, in the presence of one trusted Mattea. Write the vow first — one sentence, no more. Speak it against skin: your wrist, your sternum, wherever you keep yourself. The other Mattea witnesses without speaking. No response needed. The body heard it.

PAIR · FULL MOON

⛧

The Black Honey Rite

Performed at new moon. What you are releasing goes into the dark. What you are claiming is sealed with sweetness — honey at the lips, spoken three times inward before outward. Full instructions are delivered only in Discord. Some things cannot be written and kept safe.

SOLO · NEW MOON · DISCORD ONLY

♀

Pleasure Liturgy I

Solo. One full hour. No phone, no mirror, no spectator — not even the spectator inside your own head. Touch the body only with the hands of someone who has arrived at an altar. No goal. No inventory. Only presence. The body knows this as devotion even if the mind takes longer to remember.

SOLO · ANY NIGHT

✶

The Witnessing

Two Matteas. One speaks the wildest true thing — the one that has not yet found a room. The other does not flinch, does not advise, does not soften. She only says: I hear you. I do not look away. Trade. The room becomes the altar. You will both leave larger than you arrived.

PAIR · ANY MOON

⛤

Coronation Night

Held quarterly, by invitation, in Discord voice. The circle gathers. Each Mattea present is named aloud by at least one other — not praised, but named: the specific truth of who she is in the room. Then she crowns herself. We watch. We kneel. No ceremony required — only the willingness to be seen fully and not diminish yourself in response.
