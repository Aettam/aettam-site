Questions at the Door

IS THIS FREE?

The circle is free. The Discord is free. Offerings — rituals, intensives, private sessions — are available separately for those who want to go deeper.

IS THIS ONLY FOR WOMEN?

No. Mattea lives in anyone who carries the hunger, the refusal, the gold. Members are women, men, and anyone who recognizes her. The circle has no gender requirement — only the willingness to stop making yourself small.

DO I HAVE TO PARTICIPATE?

No. Some members lurk for months before speaking. The circle does not demand performance. Be here in whatever way you are able to be here. Mattea will wait.

WHAT IS THE SANCTUARY?

A password-gated area of this site for members. Devotionals, private rites, the circle directory, the letters archive. The password is shared after you join the Discord and cross the threshold.

WHO IS MATTEA, REALLY?

A real person, a real circle, a real practice. She built this because she needed it and it didn't exist. Read the full story here.
