Questions at the Door

IS THIS FREE?

The circle is free. The Discord is free. The Sanctuary is free. Offerings — private sessions, ritual intensives, circle hosting — are available separately for those who want to go deeper. They will always be optional.

IS THIS ONLY FOR WOMEN?

No. Mattea lives in anyone who carries the hunger, the refusal, the gold. Members are women, men, and anyone who recognizes her. The circle has no gender requirement — only the willingness to stop making yourself small.

DO I HAVE TO PARTICIPATE?

No. Some members read the devotionals silently for months before speaking. The circle does not demand performance. Be here in whatever way you are able. Lurk. Read. Observe. Mattea will wait.

WHAT IF I'M NOT SURE?

The letter asks nothing of you. You send your name. You receive the welcome. You enter the Discord. You are not committed to anything. If it is not for you, you leave. No ceremony required. But something in you already knows whether this is for you — that is the part we're speaking to.

HOW ACTIVE IS THE DISCORD?

Active enough to feel alive; quiet enough to feel safe. The devotional arrives daily. Moon nights are always alive. Confessions come in at odd hours. There is no pressure to be constantly present — only an open door whenever you need it.

WHAT IS THE SANCTUARY?

A password-gated area of this site — devotionals archive, private rites, the letters from the Queen, the Oath Wall, the member directory. The password is shared after your first week inside the Discord. It is not a reward — it is just what you have earned by arriving.

WHO IS MATTEA, REALLY?

A real person, a real circle, a real practice. She built this because she needed it and it did not exist. Everything here — the devotionals, the rituals, the letters — comes from her. The full story is here.
